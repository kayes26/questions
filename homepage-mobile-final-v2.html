FAQ

Questions parents usually ask

Who is Learn to Enlight for? +
LTE is designed for students in Pre-Cadet, Cadet College Admission, and Senior Class up to SSC/O Level. Our batches focus on structured learning, weekly exams, and the key topics students need to build strong academic performance with full parent transparency.
Are classes fully online? +
Yes. All classes are conducted online via Google Meet. Students can join from anywhere. Every class is recorded and available in the student's personal portal for rewatching.
How do parents stay updated? +
Parents receive real-time Telegram notifications for everything - exam results with feedback, homework updates, and instant notices if a class is missed or an exam is not attended. Nothing goes unnoticed.
What is the student portal? +
Each enrolled student gets a unique private dashboard where they can access all class recordings, notes and question papers, exam copies, marks, instructor feedback, and homework submissions - organized by date.
Can I pay monthly instead of full year? +
Yes. Monthly payment plans are available and are due by the 10th of each month. We also offer 3-month, 6-month, and full-year options with attractive discounts. Students who choose the full-year plan get full-year access to all materials.
Is there a referral benefit? +
Yes! If a student refers 3 new students who enroll in a single month, they get 20% off their next month's fee. Top students are also recognized and rewarded every 6 months.
How do I apply? +
Click "Apply Now" and fill in the admission form - it takes about 3 minutes. After submitting, you'll receive payment instructions. Once payment is confirmed, your child's student portal is activated.
